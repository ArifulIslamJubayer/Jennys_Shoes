Jennys Shoes Limited is a Private Limited Company represented by its Chairman and Managing Director- Mr. Nasir Khan. Jennys Shoes Limited is a renowned brand of leather shoes in Bangladesh and also in international market. Mr. Nasir Khan, a system engineer graduated from Japan, is the key person behind Jennys group. He is a Senior Vice Chairman of Leather Goods & Footwear Manufacturers & Exporters Association of Bangladesh. Mr. Nasir Khan is also a Body Member of Federation of Bangladesh Chamber of Commerce & Industries. He is the founder of all sister concerns of jennys shoes limited. He also travels many countries for business.
